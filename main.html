Why Desserts?

With the word “dessert” originating from the French word desservirt, meaning “to clear the table”, desserts has been a staple choice after clearing our tables since it’s word usage from 400 years ago. Coming in different kinds of forms and choices, let it be cakes, cookies or cupcakes, we’re pretty sure there will be one of your favorites in our long list of our favorite desserts videos!

When You Don’t Have What It Takes..

No oven? No problem! Nobody said all desserts have to be baked! In this video by Tasty, we introduce you to not one, but seven satisfying easy no-bake desserts. Ranging from absolutely chocolatey classic brigadeiros to strawberry shortcakes made entirely out of cereal, this video is truly a lifesaver for dessert lovers who are still yet to get themselves their own oven!

Tastes Good, but Sounds Good too

Somehow or another, our beloved staff found a video where you can enjoy seeing your dessert being made, while also enjoying the sound that comes with it. That’s right, lovelies! This blueberry ice cream rolls making video comes with ASMR, and as if that doesn’t already sound extremely tempting to watch, the video itself is recorded in 4K quality so you can immerse yourself fully to the thought of getting one yourself sometime soon!

What do you mean it’s fudgy?

Extremely Fudgy Brownies. What. a. TITLE. Apparently this making-of video of a fudgy brownie attempts to make a brownie that’s fudgier than your average fudgy brownies. Would they succeed to exceed your expectations of what you call a fudgy brownie? Who knows? Wouldn’t want to spoil the video for you ;)

I’d Also Like A Taste of That

Our long list of desserts would never be complete without this one : the staple one-must-try dessert called the cheesecake! If you’re a fan of cheese and everything that’s made from it, this video is definitely for you. Made from a mixture of milk, cheese, cornstarch and cream, this shiny-looking soft and jiggly cheesecake made our mouths water just by looking at it. Made us wonder if we could just get them to deliver the cake directly to our office..!

The Perfect Combination

So you meant to say that I can have both of my favorite dessert, sundae and cake, at the same time?? Yep, that right! You can get them shaped to one of the cutest animals to ever exist too! In this video by How to Cake It Step by Step, cake designer Veronica brings you a step-by-step to making an absolutely adorable Penguin Sundae Cake, which took our breath away at first sight!

So That’s How It’s Made!

Ice-cream enthusiasts? This one’s for you. In this video of an ice-cream factory, you can explore and see with your own two eyes on how a multiple kinds of ice-cream gets made, from the mixing, to shaping and coating as well. We’re pretty sure you are going to be enthralled looking at these thousands and thousands of ice-cream bars, just waiting to get themselves to your local store and into your taste buds!

Now That’s More Like It!

Not too much on the sweet stuff? Stay tuned, because desserts aren’t all about the sweet stuff. Just like life, sometimes you need some bitterness too - so that the sweeter times feels even better. For those of you who crave for the more mature side of the dessert collection, we suggest this video of an Oreo Tiramisu Cake made by the one-and-only HANSE. With a black and white finish to it’s look, this seems to be the perfect cake to enjoy while having an elegant evening tea.

Rising to the Spotlight

With all the craze for bubble tea among youngsters lately, it’s almost amazing that we have never heard of this one. Apparently, there is a dessert called Black Sugar Bubble Pavlova, which is literally a combination of bubble tea and pavlova. With the ingredients that they are using to make it, we’re pretty sure that the results are going to be both absolutely sweet and bubbly, a weird combination, but that’s definitely not going to stop us from trying it one day!

Classic Motherly Dessert

The first thing that came to my mind when I heard of this one was my mother. Souffle Pancakes, with it’s soft and fluffy finish literally reminds you of a sunny and warm morning having breakfast among the people you love. With a small number of ingredients simply mixed together and fried under low heat, maybe you too can try making some of these for the people you love!
